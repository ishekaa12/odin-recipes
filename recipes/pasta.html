Pasta Recipes

Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine, typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into various shapes. It is cooked by boiling or baking and is often served with sauces or other accompaniments.

Ingredients:
- 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, etc.)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Add the tomato sauce to the skillet and stir to combine with the garlic. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
4. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it evenly with the sauce.
6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta to absorb some of the sauce.
7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve hot.
